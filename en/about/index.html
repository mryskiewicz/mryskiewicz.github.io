About

I am a lawyer and an economist.

Currently, as part of my PhD studies at the University of Warsaw, I am conducting research on the relationship between the instability of law and its communicability.

My research interests include:

  • legal language and legalese - legal linguistics,
  • theory, practice and praxeology of legislation,
  • machine learning and statistics,
  • the use of these in the study of law,
  • philosophy of law, language and epistemology,
  • literary studies,
  • looking at language from the perspective of legal theory (anormative linguistics).
